Book Tracking App
Welcome to MyReads, your personalized bookshelf app! Organize your reading journey with ease by categorizing books into 'Currently Reading,' 'Want to Read,' and 'Read' shelves. The clean and intuitive interface lets you seamlessly move books between shelves, ensuring your virtual library reflects your reading preferences.

Discover new reads effortlessly using the search feature, where you can find and add books to your collection. The consistency between the main and search pages ensures a smooth experience, allowing you to see and manage your bookshelf seamlessly.

Navigate through your reading adventures with MyReads, where every book finds its perfect place in your digital haven. Happy reading!
